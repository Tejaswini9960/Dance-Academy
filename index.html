Our Classes

HipHop Class

We're turning up the beat and bringing the electric energy of Hip-Hop to life. Get ready to groove, pop, lock, and express yourself in a dynamic dance experience like no other.

Bharatnatyam Class

We invite you to immerse yourself in the rich and captivating world of Bharatanatyam, a classical Indian dance form that embodies grace, precision, and storytelling. Rooted in ancient traditions, Bharatanatyam is a celebration of movement, rhythm, and cultural heritage.

Kathak Class

We invite you to step into the enchanting world of Kathak, a classical Indian dance form that weaves together storytelling, rhythm, and grace. Rooted in centuries-old traditions, Kathak is a celebration of movement, expression, and cultural heritage.
